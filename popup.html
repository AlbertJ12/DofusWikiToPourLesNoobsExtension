Dofus Content Linker
This extension adds a button on any Dofus Wiki page to quickly open the corresponding guide on DofusPourLesNoobs with French names for quests, items, monsters, areas, and dungeons.
How to use:
1. Visit any page on dofuswiki.fandom.com
2. Look for the DofusPourLesNoobs button in the top-right corner
3. Click to open the guide with French name translation

Supported content:
• Quests • Items • Monsters • Areas • Subareas • Dungeons
